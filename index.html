About DDB EVENTS

With over 15 years of experience, DDB EVENTS has grown into one of India’s trusted names in event planning and management. What began as a passion for creating memorable celebrations has evolved into a full-service event company that delivers excellence, elegance, and emotion in every event we plan. From the vibrant traditions of Big Fat Indian weddings to the sharp precision of corporate events, and the joy of private parties, birthdays, and family milestones, we bring creativity, professionalism, and a personal touch to every occasion. Whether it's planning a destination wedding in Jaipur, a themed birthday in Mumbai, or a corporate awards night in Bangalore, our expert team handles everything — from venue selection, décor, and entertainment to guest coordination and on-site execution. At the heart of every event we do is one simple mission: to turn your vision into a beautiful, stress-free reality. With custom-tailored solutions, innovative ideas, and a deep understanding of Indian culture and celebration styles, we are here to create experiences that are not only successful but unforgettable. Let us bring your next celebration to life — with heart, harmony, and a touch of grandeur.
